Website Project


Thundersam



Overview

For the Thundersam company website, my focus was on creating an engaging online platform for a local graphic design company to showcase their work collection. Developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this project combines functionality with aesthetics to introduce the company's graphic designing skills and projects in a captivating manner.

Project Highlights
  • Collaborated closely with the company, adjusting designs to align with their preferences and brand identity.
  • Developed a responsive website, ensuring optimal viewing for users across various devices other than a pc, such as iPad or a mobile phone.
  • Integrated a contact form that facilitates direct communication, sending emails to the company for inquiries and collaboration opportunities.
  • Integrated HTML for structural integrity, CSS for visually appealing styling according to the company's theme, and JavaScript for interactive animations to form a dynamic website.
Learning Outcomes

This project not only enhanced my coding skills but also exposed me to the intricacies of web design. Through the development process, I delved into UI/UX principles, tailoring the website to meet user and company needs. Responsive design ensures optimal viewing across devices, while the contact form provides a seamless way for users to connect with Thundersam.

Designing this portfolio website expanded my understanding of user-centric design, emphasizing the importance of a visually appealing and functional interface in creating a positive online presence.
